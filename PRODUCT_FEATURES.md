# Product Features & Sales Playbook

This document tracks the advanced AI and UEM features built into our platform. It is designed for the Sales and Go-To-Market (GTM) teams to effectively demonstrate the unique business value of the platform.

---

## 🚀 Currently Implemented Features

### 1. Real-Time DEX Telemetry & Campaigns
* **What it is:** A lightweight desktop agent that continuously monitors hardware health, active application crashes (via Windows Event Logs), and security posture (BitLocker, Firewall, Defender). It allows admins to run automated PowerShell remediation campaigns.
* **Business Value:** Reduces Mean Time to Resolution (MTTR) by allowing IT to fix issues remotely in bulk, often before the user submits a ticket.
* **How to Sell It:** Show the prospect the DEX dashboard. Point out that unlike traditional ITSM which waits for a user to complain, our platform proactively highlights exactly which devices are failing right now. Run a "Campaign" live to show how 1,000 devices can be fixed with one click.

### 2. Module-Level Access Control (RBAC)
* **What it is:** Granular permissions allowing admins to assign specific toolsets (Self-Service, DEX, Service Desk, Asset Mgmt) to individual users.
* **Business Value:** Streamlines the UI for employees, ensuring data security and minimizing training time by hiding irrelevant IT tools.
* **How to Sell It:** Show the User Management dashboard. Toggle off the "DEX" module for a test user, switch screens, and show how clean the employee portal becomes. Emphasize compliance and security.

## 🏗️ Roadmap (The "Next-Gen" Features)

The following features are approved for development. They represent our core competitive moat against legacy platforms.

### 1. Shadow IT Cost-Killer
* **Concept:** The DEX agent tracks foreground application/browser usage to identify unused, paid SaaS licenses.
* **Sales Pitch:** "Our software pays for itself in 60 days. We automatically find and revoke SaaS licenses your employees aren't actually using."

### 2. Predictive Burnout & Sentiment Engine
* **Concept:** Correlating high hardware stress (e.g., 100% CPU) with frustrated ticket sentiment to flag employees at risk of quitting.
* **Sales Pitch:** "We don't just save computers; we save your best people. We warn HR when an employee is burning out due to technological frustration."

### 3. P2P AI Knowledge Swarm (Optional)
* **Concept:** Bypassing IT by automatically routing simple tickets to a coworker who successfully resolved the exact same issue recently.
* **Sales Pitch:** "Cut your L1 Helpdesk costs by 30%. Our AI builds a peer-to-peer support network automatically."

### 4. Hardware Lifecycle Arbitrage
* **Concept:** Using real hardware telemetry to predict exactly when a laptop will fail, and alerting IT at the perfect mathematical moment to sell it on the refurbished market.
* **Sales Pitch:** "Stop replacing laptops based on arbitrary 3-year cycles. We tell you the exact day to sell a laptop to maximize your resale ROI before the battery permanently degrades."

### 5. The "Digital Twin" IT Agent (Powered by Vector DB)
* **Concept:** Ingesting the entire ticket resolution history of your best senior IT agent into a Vector Database to create an AI persona that troubleshoots exactly like them.
* **Sales Pitch:** "Clone your best IT veteran. When they go on vacation or retire, their exact diagnostic logic and bedside manner lives on in our AI."

### 6. Self-Healing Smart Contracts
* **Concept:** Proactive triggers that run remote scripts automatically (e.g., if RAM > 95% and not in a Zoom meeting, clear memory).
* **Sales Pitch:** "Welcome to Zero-Ticket IT. We resolve 40% of issues before your employee even knows there's a problem."

### 7. Visual AI Zero-Click Onboarding
* **Concept:** An AI agent that visually reads the screen and clicks through complex legacy software installers for new hires.
* **Sales Pitch:** "True zero-touch deployment. Ship a laptop to a new hire, and watch as our AI visually navigates the desktop to set up their complex legacy software."
